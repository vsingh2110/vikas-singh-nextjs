## üìß Step-by-Step Email Sending Tutorial

### Step 1: Open the Email Template

You will receive the email template from your manager or team lead. Open that email in your inbox (Gmail or Google Workspace).

**What you'll see:**
- A professionally designed invitation with Phantom Healthcare branding
- The IRIA 2026 event details
- Our stall number (D-2)
- Contact information and social media links

### Step 2: Select and Copy the Email Content

‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL: Follow this EXACTLY**

1. **DO NOT use Ctrl+A** (Select All) - This will copy headers, subject line, and reply buttons which will break the formatting

2. **Use your mouse to manually select:**
   - Start from the TOP of the email content (just below the subject line area)
   - Drag down to the BOTTOM of the email content (just above the Reply/Forward buttons)
   - Your selection should include ONLY the email body content

3. **Copy the selected content:**
   - Press **Ctrl+C** (Windows) or **Cmd+C** (Mac)
   - Or Right-click ‚Üí Copy

### Step 3: Compose a New Email

1. Click **Compose** (Gmail) or **New Email** (other clients)
2. **IMPORTANT: Remove your email signature first!**
   - Click on the signature area at the bottom
   - Delete it completely
   - Our email template already has all contact information

### Step 4: Paste the Email Template

1. Click inside the email body area
2. Press **Ctrl+V** (Windows) or **Cmd+V** (Mac) to paste
3. The entire responsive email should appear with all formatting intact

**Why this works:** Our template is designed using Beefree and copied from preview mode, which preserves responsiveness even in Gmail/Outlook compose windows.

### Step 5: Add Recipients Using BCC

‚ö†Ô∏è **VERY IMPORTANT: Always use BCC!**

**Why BCC?**
- Protects client privacy - recipients cannot see each other's email addresses
- Looks professional - each recipient thinks they received a personal email
- Required for GDPR/privacy compliance
- Prevents "Reply All" disasters

**How to add BCC recipients:**

1. Click on **BCC** field (next to "To" field)
2. Add recipient email addresses separated by commas
3. Leave the **"To"** field empty OR add your own email address
4. **Recommended:** Put your own email in "To" field to track delivery

### Step 6: Add Subject Line

Use a clear, professional subject line:

**Recommended Subject Lines:**
- `Invitation: Meet Phantom Healthcare at IRIA 2026 - Stall D-2`
- `Join us at IRIA 2026 | Phantom Healthcare - Stall D-2, HITEX Hyderabad`
- `Special Invitation: 78th IRIA Conference - 29 Jan to 1 Feb 2026`

### Step 7: Send in Batches

‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL: Follow batch limits to avoid spam filters!**

| Email Account Type | Max per Batch | Wait Between Batches | Daily Limit |
|--------------------|---------------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Free Gmail** (@gmail.com) | 10-15 | 15-20 minutes | 100-150/day |
| **Google Workspace** (@phantomhealthcare.com) | 15-20 | 15 minutes | 2000/day |

**Batch Sending Process:**

1. **Batch 1:** Add 10-15 BCC recipients ‚Üí Send
2. **Wait 15-20 minutes** (do other work)
3. **Batch 2:** Add next 10-15 BCC recipients ‚Üí Send
4. **Repeat** until all contacts are covered
5. **Track:** Keep a list of who you've sent to

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Email looks broken after pasting?

- Make sure you selected ONLY the email body content
- Don't use Ctrl+A - use mouse selection
- Try pasting in a new compose window
- Check if signature was removed before pasting

### Email going to spam?

- Send in smaller batches (5-10)
- Increase wait time between batches (30 min)
- Add a personal line at the top like "Dear [Name],"
- Avoid spam trigger words in subject line

### Images not showing?

- This is normal for first view in some email clients
- Recipients need to click "Display images" once
- All images are properly hosted and will load

### Formatting lost?

- Re-copy from the original template email
- Make sure you're pasting in HTML mode (not plain text)
- Gmail sometimes auto-converts - try again
